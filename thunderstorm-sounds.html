Thunderstorm Sounds

Not a 10-hour YouTube loop. Real-time synthesized thunder that never repeats — each rumble is unique, each crack is different. This storm is alive.

Start Listening

Thunderstorms are nature's noise machine. The combination of rain, wind, and unpredictable thunder creates a sound profile that's both immersive and sleep-inducing. Your brain interprets it as "stay inside, stay safe, let go."

A Storm That Never Repeats

Most thunderstorm recordings loop every few minutes — you start hearing the pattern, and the magic breaks. Drift's thunder is generated in real-time: each crack swells and decays at random intensity, at random intervals. You can listen for hours and never hear the same thunder twice.

Storm Mixes

Runs in Your Browser. Works Offline.

No app. No account. No ads interrupting your storm at 3am. Drift's audio engine runs entirely in your browser using the Web Audio API. After the first load, it even works without internet. The storm goes on.
