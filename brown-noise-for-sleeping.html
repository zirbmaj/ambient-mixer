Start Listening

Brown noise emphasizes lower frequencies — it sounds like a deep rumble, a distant waterfall, or heavy wind. Unlike white noise (which can feel harsh), brown noise feels warm and enveloping. It's become the go-to for people who need help falling asleep or staying asleep.
