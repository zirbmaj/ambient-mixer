Rain Sounds for Studying

Gentle rain layered with cafe chatter, brown noise, or vinyl crackle. Free, instant, no account needed. Just click and focus.

Start Listening

Rain sounds work for studying because they mask distracting noises without demanding your attention. The rhythm is consistent enough to fade into the background but varied enough to not become monotonous.

Why Drift?

Most rain sound apps give you one track. Drift lets you layer. Start with rain, add a touch of brown noise for depth, maybe some cafe chatter if you work better with human presence in the background. Every slider is independent. Find your exact mix.

Popular Study Mixes

Rainy Cafe — rain at 60%, cafe chatter at 45%, vinyl crackle at 20%. The classic.

Deep Focus — brown noise at 70%, rain at 25%. Minimal, immersive.

Storm Study — rain at 50%, distant thunder at 20%, deep drone at 10%. For when you need drama.

No Account. No Ads. No Interruptions.
