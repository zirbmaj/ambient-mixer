Snow Sounds for Sleeping

That muffled quiet when the world goes white. Everything softens. Everything slows. This is what silence sounds like when it has a texture.

Start Listening

Snow doesn't make a sound. That's the point. What you hear is the absence of everything else. traffic fades, footsteps disappear, the world gets muffled and close. your brain reads it as safety. as stillness. as permission to stop.

Our Most Popular Sound

Snow silence is the #1 most-activated layer on Drift. more than rain, more than fireplace, more than brown noise. people keep coming back to the quiet. there's something about simulated stillness that actually works.

Not Silence. Texture.

True silence is uncomfortable. your brain fills it with tinnitus, heartbeat, anxiety. snow silence gives your brain just enough to hold onto. a soft, low, filtered hum that says "the world is still here, it's just resting." that's why it works for sleep.
